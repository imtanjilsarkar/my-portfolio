About

A builder who
codes with purpose.

I'm a Computer Science & Engineering student at Independent University, Bangladesh (IUB), with a deep interest in software development, algorithms, and turning real problems into digital solutions.

Whether it's building a blood bank system, creating a tutor marketplace, or developing Chrome extensions — I focus on things that actually matter to people. I've tutored 100+ university students across CSE and STEM, and I'm currently working toward research and studying abroad.

When I'm not coding, I'm leading the Millennium Fellowship campus chapter or ideating on my AI startup, PIVOLVE.
